Early Life:

I was born in a small city called Jackson, Tennessee in 1991 to my parents John and Dianne. We lived in Tennessee with my brother Adam who was born in 1993 until I was 13 when we moved to South Carolina. I enjoyed South Carolina more than Tennessee, the area of Tennessee we lived in was mostly cotton fields...so there wasn't much to do.

In South Carolina I went to a high school called TL Hanna High School. Turns out, the school is famous because of a Hollywood movie called "Radio" starring Cuba Gooding Jr. was made about the school and the story of Radio, a football manager who had to overcome adversity and become accepted at the school. It is a really good movie and recommend it to everyone.
